What are the most reliable generator brands in Kenya?

Some of the most reliable generator brands in Kenya include Hisaki, Aico, and Honda. These brands are known for their high-quality components, advanced technology, and long-lasting performance.

What is KVA, and how does it relate to generator power output?

KVA stands for kilovolt-ampere, and it is a unit of electrical power. Generator power output is typically measured in KVA, and it represents the maximum amount of power that the generator can produce at any given time.

What should I consider when choosing a generator in Kenya?

When choosing a generator in Kenya, there are several factors to consider, including the power output (in KVA), the fuel type (such as petrol or diesel), the brand and model, the intended use (such as residential or industrial), and the price.

What are some common performance issues with generators?

Some common performance issues with generators include starting problems, low power output, fuel efficiency issues, and overheating. These issues can often be prevented or resolved through proper maintenance and care.

How often should I service my generator?

It is recommended that you service your generator at least once per year, or more frequently if it is used heavily or in harsh conditions. Regular maintenance can help prevent performance issues and extend the life of your generator.

Can I use a generator indoors?

No, it is not safe to use a generator indoors due to the risk of carbon monoxide poisoning. Generators should only be used in well-ventilated outdoor areas, away from doors and windows.

How long can a generator run continuously?

The length of time a generator can run continuously depends on its fuel capacity and the amount of power being used. Most generators can run continuously for several hours to several days, depending on their size and fuel efficiency. It is important to refer to the manufacturer's guidelines for the specific generator being used.
